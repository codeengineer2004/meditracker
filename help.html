user guidance

  • Select the initial webpage.
  • Provide details on ailments or medications.
  • After that, it will describe the associated illness.
  • Select the second webpage.
  • Provide the relevant email address.
  • After that, it will send an email that can be set as a reminder.
  • Press the link to the third page.
  • Enter the name and dosage of the prescribed medication in the time remaining field.
  • At the time scheduled, the alaram will then generate a buzzing sound.
